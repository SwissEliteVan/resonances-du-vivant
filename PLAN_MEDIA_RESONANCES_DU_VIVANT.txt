VIDEO A - ALAIN MOURET: LE TRAIT
Titre: "Minimalist Abstract Geometry with Golden Light Ray"

Full Prompt (English - Cinematic Vocabulary):

A meticulously composed minimalist digital artwork unfolds at a glacial pace over 
forty seconds. The composition begins in absolute black, void of all visual information. 
The camera remains static on an ethereal plane, observing a sparse landscape of pure 
geometry. Gradually, a solitary ray of warm golden light—resembling liquid brass or 
molten gold—enters the frame from the upper left quadrant, traveling on a diagonal 
trajectory with fluid, organic motion. The light illuminates simple, cleanly executed 
geometric forms: thin lines, subtle arcs, and minimalist rectangles rendered in pure 
black against the darkness. The digital texture mimics hand-drawn precision, with 
visible line weights that suggest intentional artistic control. The golden ray caresses 
each form without overwhelming them, casting delicate shadows that add depth. The 
progression follows a slow fade-in tempo, creating atmospheric tension and contemplative 
mood. Color palette: deepest black (RGB 0,0,0), warm metallic gold (RGB 220,160,40), 
and near-imperceptible charcoal gradients. No audio. Aspect ratio 16:9. The overall 
sensation conveys mystery, artistic mastery, and the revelation of invisible beauty 
through light. Style reference: minimalist architecture photography merged with 
contemporary digital abstraction.

VIDEO B - AMBIANCE HERO: IMMERSIVE EXHIBITION PASSAGE
Titre: "Fluorescent Gallery Experience - Looping Atmospheric Journey"

Full Prompt (English - Cinematic Vocabulary):

A hypnotic fifty-five-second immersive journey through a contemporary art exhibition 
space transitions from sophisticated darkness into revelation and back again, designed 
to loop infinitely without visible seams. The composition employs dynamic lighting 
cinematography with ultraviolet light as the protagonist. The sequence begins in near 
total darkness—gallery lighting at minimal intensity. Silhouetted geometric installations 
occupy the architectural space: abstract sculptures, suspended textile compositions, and 
wall-mounted panel arrays rendered as pristine black forms against marginally lighter 
backgrounds. Suddenly, strategic ultraviolet light sources ignite, illuminating hidden 
fluorescent pigments embedded within the artworks. These materials emit otherworldly 
luminescence—electric magenta, vibrant cyan, neon violet, and radioactive lime—creating 
striking chromatic contrast against the sombre environment. The camera glides through 
the gallery space with deliberate, ethereal motion, revealing installations sequentially. 
The lighting rhythm alternates: darkness gives way to UV revelation, then retreats back 
into shadow, creating cyclical waves of discovery. No visible gallery visitors. The 
texture appears ultra-realistic, capturing dust motes catching UV light, slight 
environmental haze, and the volumetric quality of ultraviolet illumination. The 
atmosphere remains luxurious, mysterious, and deeply immersive. Color palette emphasizes 
extreme contrast: pure black (RGB 0,0,0) warmed with deep charcoal grays (RGB 20,20,20), 
contrasting vividly against fluorescent neon ranges (magenta RGB 255,0,255, cyan RGB 0,255,255). 
No audio. Aspect ratio 16:9. The looping must conclude with smooth transition that 
reprises the opening darkness seamlessly. Overall aesthetic: high-end gallery experience 
merged with immersive installation art, conveying sophistication, revelation, and wonder.

Attributs critiques de la balise video:
- autoplay: Lecture automatique au chargement
- muted: Son desactive (REQUIS pour compatibility autoplay moderne)

- preload="auto": Precharge le video pour fluidite
- poster: Image de secours avant chargement/lecture

- resonances-du-vivant-alain-mouret-le-trait-poster-1200.webp

Regles SEO:
- Noms en minuscules avec traits d'union uniquement
- Inclure nom de l'artiste pour pertinence semantique
- Inclure description du contenu (artist, hero, ambiance, etc.)
- Numeroter resolutions pour clarté (600, 1200, 1600)
- Extension explicite du format (.mp4, .webm, .webp)
- Eviter caracteres speciaux, accents, underscores

B. FORMATS VIDEO OPTIMISES:

Format 1 - MP4 H.264 (Compatibilite universelle):
- Codec video: H.264 (AVC)
- Profil: Main Profile, Level 4.0
- Debit video: 3000-4500 kbps (1080p), 1500-2500 kbps (720p)
- Taux de rafraichissement: 24fps ou 30fps
- Audio: AUCUN (silence ou suppression complete)
- Extension: .mp4

Format 2 - WebM VP9 (Compression optimale sans audio):
- Codec video: VP9
- Profil: Profile 0
- Debit video: 1500-2000 kbps (1080p), 800-1200 kbps (720p)
- Taux de rafraichissement: 24fps
- Audio: AUCUN (fichier audio remove)
- Extension: .webm

Format 3 - Poster/Thumbnail (Image statique):
- Format: WebP pour compression moderne
- Alternative fallback: JPEG
- Codec: WebP VP8
- Resolution: Cf. section dimensions

Priorite de service au navigateur:
1. WebM VP9 sans audio (poids minimum, optimal)
2. MP4 H.264 sans audio (fallback compatibilite)

C. DIMENSIONS ET RESOLUTIONS:

Resolution 1 - Escales (Video articles 16:9):
- 1920x1080px (Full HD) - Resolution de reference
- 1280x720px (HD) - Mobile/tablet
- 854x480px (480p) - Mobile legacy
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (constant)
- Poster: 1200x675px (WebP recommande)

Resolution 2 - Hero Background (Video fullscreen 16:9):
- 1920x1080px (Full HD) - Viewport standard
- 2560x1440px (2K) - Large screens/high-DPI
- 1280x720px (HD) - Mobile optimization
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 (constant)
- Poster (hero): 1920x1080px (WebP recommande)

D. SPECIFICATIONS AUDIO:

Video Ambiance Hero SANS AUDIO:
- Remove toute piste audio PCM
- Reduction poids fichier: -30 a -50% comparé a version avec son
- Avantages accessibilite: Permet audio personnalisé ou son ambiant site

Videos Escales (optionnel son ambiant):
- Si musique/ambiance: Codec audio AAC
- Bitrate audio: 128-192 kbps maximum
- Frequence: 44.1 kHz
- Recommandation: Rester mutes pour coherence design

E. COMPRESSION ET OPTIMISATION:

MP4 H.264 sans audio (Exemple commande ffmpeg):
ffmpeg -i input-video.mov -c:v libx264 -preset medium -crf 23 -b:v 3500k -vf scale=1920:1080 -r 24 -an output-video.mp4

WebM VP9 sans audio (Exemple commande ffmpeg):
ffmpeg -i input-video.mov -c:v libvpx-vp9 -b:v 1800k -preset 4 -vf scale=1920:1080 -r 24 -an output-video.webm
